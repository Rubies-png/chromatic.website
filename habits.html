Habits

  1. Mindfulness Meditation
    Why? Because practicing mindfulness meditation each day improves focus and awareness by expanding my mind.

  2. Gratitude Journaling
    3. Why? Because by writing down down and documenting up to three things you're grateful for each day cultivates a positive mindset.

  3. Reading
    4. Why? Because by reading for up to 30 minutes each day expands our knowledge, imagination exposing us to creative thinking and different emotions.

  4. Eye Excercises
    5. Why? Because performing eye excercises daily improves my focus and reduce eye strain, keeping me at peak performance in my work.

  5. Observation Practice
    6. Why? Because practicing observation of my surroundings without judgement helps improve my perception on various matters.

  6. Learning a New Skill
    7. Why? Because as an artist, learning, engaging and immersing myself in a new skill helps me to challenge my perception and sight.

  7. Imagination Practice
    8. Why? Because by setting aside time each day to think and imagine helps my immagination run wild.
